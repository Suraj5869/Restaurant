About Us

----- Since 2000 -----

Foody Katta is build and grow till here on the customers love and great staff of restaurant. We provides a good services since 2000. The restaurant is a real gourmet’s paradise. Its beginnings can be traced back to the year 2000. We give fresh and delicious food. Foody Katta Restaurant with its chef Janez Bratovž was the first restaurant to make the World’s 50 Best Restaurants.

There are total 20 branches at different places, all the brach structures are designed by the greatest Slovenian architect. Even though it is located right in the middle of the city, it offers a calm and elegant escape where our guests can relax and peacefully experience exquisite climate. Clean lines and the perfection of the interior decoration are a special experience for many tourists who cannot wait to visit us again.
